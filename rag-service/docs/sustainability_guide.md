# Sustainability and Green Operations Guide — MetLife Stadium

This guide outlines waste-sorting rules, location of recycling and water-refill stations, and green operating protocols for fans and stadium staff.

## 1. Waste Sorting and Bins Layout
MetLife Stadium is committed to zero-waste goals. We use a three-stream waste disposal system across the entire venue:
- **Blue Bins (Recycling)**:
  - **Acceptable**: Empty aluminum cans, plastic cups, clean plastic bottles, and dry cardboard food containers.
  - **Prohibited**: Liquid waste, food scraps, greasy pizza boxes.
- **Green Bins (Compost)**:
  - **Acceptable**: All food leftovers, paper napkins, and certified compostable food trays/plates/cutlery provided by concession stands.
- **Black Bins (Landfill Trash)**:
  - **Acceptable**: Foil-lined packaging, single-use condiments packets, and diapers.

## 2. Waste Bin Overflow Protocol (Staff Rules)
To maintain cleanliness and support sorting integrity, staff must monitor bin capacities:
1. **Regular Checks**: Concourse volunteers should inspect waste stations in their designated section every 30 minutes.
2. **Threshold for Service**: If any bin is observed to be **more than 80% full**, it must be reported immediately.
3. **Reporting Procedure**: Alert Stadium Environmental Services (SES) via radio Channel 4 or report the bin section number (e.g., "Section 112 waste station recycling bin near capacity") in the Volunteer Dashboard.
4. **Interim Mitigation**: If an SES team is delayed, staff should tie off the full bag, place a fresh bag inside the bin from the station supply cabinet, and place the tied bag in the designated local service corridor for pickup.

## 3. Water Refill Stations
To reduce single-use plastic waste, fans are encouraged to bring their own bottles and utilize filtration stations:
- **Bottle Policy**: Fans are permitted to bring empty, clear, reusable plastic water bottles (up to 20 oz / 600 ml) into the stadium. Metal, glass, or insulated vacuum bottles are prohibited for security reasons.
- **Station Locations**: Dual water-refill and drinking fountains are located on the main concourse walkways near:
  - **100 Level (Lower)**: Sections 108, 122, 134, and 146.
  - **300 Level (Upper)**: Sections 309, 321, and 338.

## 4. Energy-Saving Protocols for Post-Event Staff
Within 30 minutes of the final whistle or event conclusion, staff must execute energy conservation steps:
- **Concessions Power-Down**: Concession operators must power down all non-essential equipment: grills, fryers, food-warmers, and digital menu screens. Keep only walk-in refrigerators and safety lighting active.
- **Concourse Light Dimming**: Operations operators will dim public concourse lights to 30% capacity once the last fan sweeps are complete.
- **Thermal Standby**: Shift HVAC controls in luxury suites, meeting rooms, and empty administrative offices into "Eco-Standby" mode (78°F / 25°C for cooling; 65°F / 18°C for heating).
