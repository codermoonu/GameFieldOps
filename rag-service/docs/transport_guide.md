# Transport and Transit Guide — MetLife Stadium

This guide contains official information regarding parking lot allocations, rideshare zones, public shuttle services, and train schedules for event days at MetLife Stadium.

## 1. Parking Lots and Vehicle Zones
Parking facilities surrounding the stadium are color-coded and numbered for easy identification:
- **Lot 1 (South Lot)**: Located directly outside Gate D. This lot contains the highest concentration of accessible (ADA) parking spaces. A valid state-issued placard is required for entry.
- **Lot 3 (West Lot)**: Located outside Gate A. This is the **exclusive Rideshare Drop-off/Pick-up Zone** (e.g., Uber, Lyft). General public parking is prohibited here during major events to keep roads clear for passenger drop-offs.
- **Lot 4 (Northwest Lot)**: General parking area. Tailgating is permitted in this lot within designated boundaries.
- **Lot 5 (North Lot)**: Located outside Gate B. This lot contains the **Shuttle Bus Depot** and charter bus parking. It is closed to private commuter vehicles.

## 2. Shuttle Bus Services and Schedules
Shuttle service runs continuously on event days to transport fans from parking lots and local transit hubs to the stadium gates.
- **Stadium Perimeter Shuttle (Main Stadium Loop)**:
  - **Route**: Loops clockwise stopping at Lot 1 (Gate D) -> Lot 3 (Gate A) -> Lot 5 (Gate B) -> Transit Plaza (Gate C).
  - **Frequency**: Every 8–10 minutes.
  - **Hours**: Starts 3 hours before event kickoff and operates until 2 hours after the event concludes.
  - **Accessibility**: All shuttle buses are equipped with low floors and hydraulic ramps to accommodate wheelchairs.
- **Off-Site Express Shuttle**:
  - **Route**: Connects off-site park-and-ride facilities directly to the Shuttle Bus Depot in Lot 5.
  - **Frequency**: Every 15 minutes.

## 3. Public Transit Connections
Public transport is highly recommended to avoid heavy traffic congestion:
- **MetLife Stadium Rail Station (Train Service)**:
  - **Location**: Situated on the east side of the stadium, within a 3-minute walking distance of **Gate C**.
  - **Service**: Connects directly to Secaucus Junction. Trains operate starting 3.5 hours before the event, running every 10–12 minutes. Post-game trains run continuously for 2 hours after the event ends.
- **Coach USA Express Bus Service (351 Express)**:
  - **Location**: Operates between New York City Port Authority Bus Terminal (Gate 305) and the Shuttle Bus Depot in **Lot 5**.
  - **Service**: Continuous departures starting 2.5 hours before kickoff, with return buses running until 1 hour after the game ends.
- **Bicycle Racks**: Located near the outer security checkpoints of Gate A and Gate C.
