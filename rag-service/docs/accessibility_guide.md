# Accessibility and Inclusion Guide — MetLife Stadium

This guide outlines accessibility features, wheelchair navigation paths, sensory services, and companion seating policies at MetLife Stadium for matchday operations.

## 1. Wheelchair Routes and General Access
All entry gates (Gate A, B, C, and D) are fully ADA accessible, featuring level ground or low-incline ramps.
- **Ramp Locations**: The main ramps are situated on the west side (near Gate A) and the east side (near Gate C). Ramps are designed with 1:12 slopes and resting landings every 9 meters.
- **Concourse Navigation**: The main lower concourse (100 Level) and upper concourse (300 Level) are flat, wide, and paved with smooth concrete. There are no steps or structural curbs along the main walkways.

## 2. Elevator Locations and Access Areas
Elevator banks are available to transport guests with limited mobility or wheelchair requirements between levels:
- **Gate A (West Side)**: The **West Elevator Bank** is located adjacent to Section 114. It services the Lower Concourse, Club Level, Suite Level, and Upper Concourse.
- **Gate B (North Side)**: The **North Elevator Bank** is located adjacent to Section 127. It services the Lower Concourse and Upper Concourse.
- **Gate C (East Side)**: The **East Elevator Bank** is located adjacent to Section 142. It services the Lower Concourse, East Club Level, and Upper Concourse.
- **Gate D (South Side)**: The **South Elevator Bank** is located adjacent to Section 103. It services the Lower Concourse and Upper Concourse.

## 3. Accessible Restrooms
All public restrooms at MetLife Stadium contain ADA-compliant wheelchair-accessible stalls.
- **Single-Stall/All-Gender Accessible Restrooms**: For families or guests requiring assistance, private, single-stall accessible restrooms are located near the following sections:
  - **100 Level (Lower)**: Sections 105, 117, 129, and 140.
  - **200 Level (Club/Suite)**: Sections 207, 219, and 235.
  - **300 Level (Upper)**: Sections 303, 318, 330, and 345.
- All accessible restrooms feature grab bars, lowered sinks, automatic soap dispensers, and emergency call buttons.

## 4. Sensory-Friendly Rooms and Kits
MetLife Stadium is certified sensory-inclusive. We provide amenities for fans with autism, dementia, PTSD, or other sensory needs:
- **Dedicated Sensory Room**: Located on the Plaza Level near **Section 119**. The room is soundproofed and features soft lighting, sensory games, bubble walls, and beanbag seating.
- **Sensory Kits**: Guests can borrow a sensory bag containing noise-canceling headphones, fidget tools, and communication cards. Kits are available at Guest Services booths located near:
  - **Section 112** (West Side, near Gate A)
  - **Section 138** (East Side, near Gate C)
- No deposits are required, but a valid ID must be left at the booth while the kit is in use.

## 5. Companion Seating Policy
- **Seating Allocations**: Accessible wheelchair spaces are available in all price categories throughout the stadium. Each wheelchair space has adjacent companion seats.
- **Companion Limit**: Under standard policy, fans may purchase up to 3 companion tickets per wheelchair seat.
- **On-Day Relocations**: If a fan arrives with a ticket for standard seating but requires accessible seating, they should report to the Guest Services Booths at Section 112 or 138. Relocations are subject to availability on a first-come, first-served basis.
